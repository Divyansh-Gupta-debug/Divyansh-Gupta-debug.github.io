2. Description of Service

Classroom Plus is a free Chrome extension that enhances the Google Classroom experience by providing search, bookmark, and navigation features for class posts, announcements, assignments, and course materials. The Extension accesses Google Classroom data through the Google Classroom API with your explicit permission.

3. Google Account Authorization

To function, the Extension requires read-only access to your Google Classroom data through OAuth 2.0 authorization. By granting access, you permit the Extension to:

You can revoke this access at any time through your Google Account permissions.
